mongoimport | imports content from an Extended JSON, CSV, or TSV export :

mongoimport --uri mongodb+srv://jananisekar2104:<PASSWORD>@yogamaster.ydqio.mongodb.net/<DATABASE> --collection <COLLECTION> --type <FILETYPE> --file <FILENAME>


mongoexport | produces a JSON or CSV export of data stored in a MongoDB instance:

mongoexport --uri mongodb+srv://jananisekar2104:<PASSWORD>@yogamaster.ydqio.mongodb.net/<DATABASE> --collection <COLLECTION> --type <FILETYPE> --out <FILENAME>
